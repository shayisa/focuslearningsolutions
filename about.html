Our Story

Three decades of transforming leaders and organisations across Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Since 1996

Built on Passion.
Driven by Impact.

Focus Learning Solutions was founded in 1996 in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia, with a singular vision: to help organisations unlock the full potential of their people.

What started as a focused consultancy has grown into one of Malaysia's most trusted corporate training firms, serving over 500 organisations across 14 countries and 17 industry verticals.

For nearly three decades, we've partnered with HR directors, L&D managers, and C-suite leaders to design and deliver programmes that create lasting behavioural change — not just classroom learning.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1996
Focus Learning Solutions founded in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia, by Ahmad Jaidi Tajuddin and Abdul Malik Abdul Rahman.
Early 2000s
Expanded from training delivery to full organisational consulting. Began partnerships with major GLCs including PETRONAS and Maybank.
2005
Launched proprietary leadership frameworks (WTL, BEST) and expanded into the Middle East market — Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and UAE.
2010
Achieved HRDF 5-Star Rating and recognized as a Petronas Top Leading Vendor. Extended reach into Southeast Asia and Africa.
2015
Surpassed 500 client organisations milestone. Launched the Service From The Heart (SFTH) programme, becoming a benchmark in customer experience training for banking and healthcare.
2020
Pivoted to hybrid and virtual delivery models. Expanded consulting arm under Focus Learning Consulting led by Nur Nadira.
2024 - Present
Operating across 14 countries with 17 industry verticals. Continuing to evolve with digital-first learning solutions while maintaining our signature hands-on, experiential approach.
