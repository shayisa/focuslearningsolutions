Partner with our consultants for organisational development and strategic HR transformation.

The Challenge

Sound Familiar?

You need an outside perspective. Internal HR is overwhelmed. Strategy is clear but execution is lagging. You need expertise you don't have in-house. And the stakes are too high for trial and error.

Our Approach

Your Strategic Partner, Not Just a Vendor

Our consulting engagements go beyond recommendations on paper. We embed ourselves in your organisation to understand the real challenges, politics, and dynamics that shape execution. Then we partner with your leadership team to design and implement solutions that actually work.

With three decades of experience across 17 industry verticals, we bring a depth of practical knowledge that academic consultants cannot match. Our consultants have sat in the seats your leaders sit in -- they understand the pressures of running a business, not just the theory of improving one.

From strategic talent planning to organisational restructuring, our consulting arm (Focus Learning Consulting) provides the external expertise and objectivity that every organisation needs at critical moments of growth or change.

Our Services

Strategic Consulting Services

Comprehensive consulting solutions that bridge the gap between strategy and execution.

STEP Consulting

Strategic Talent & Engagement Planning. A comprehensive organisational review and HR strategy development engagement. We assess your current talent landscape, identify gaps, and build a strategic roadmap for human capital development that aligns with business objectives.

Organisational Development

Structure design, competency frameworks, performance management systems, and talent mapping. We help you design the organisational architecture that enables your strategy -- from reporting lines to role definitions to career pathways.

Training Needs Analysis (TNA)

Systematic assessment of learning gaps, ROI-focused training plans, and competency benchmarking. Before you invest in training, we ensure you're investing in the right areas. Our TNA process identifies precise skill gaps and designs a development roadmap that maximises return on your training budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Engagement duration varies based on scope. A Training Needs Analysis typically takes 4-8 weeks. Organisational development projects range from 3-6 months. STEP Consulting (comprehensive strategic review) is usually a 6-12 month engagement with phased deliverables. We always begin with a scoping discussion to understand your needs and provide a clear timeline and investment before starting.
Every consulting engagement produces tangible, actionable deliverables. These typically include diagnostic reports with data-driven findings, strategic recommendations with implementation roadmaps, framework documents (competency models, performance systems, training plans), and presentation decks for board or leadership team alignment. We also provide implementation support -- not just recommendations -- to ensure execution.
Training programmes develop people skills through workshops and experiential learning. Consulting addresses organisational systems, structures, and strategies. Think of it this way: training teaches your managers how to lead; consulting designs the leadership framework, succession plan, and performance system that enables them to succeed. Many clients use both -- consulting to design the strategy, and training to build the capability.
