Transform your service culture and elevate every customer touchpoint.

The Challenge

Sound Familiar?

Customer expectations are rising. Your NPS scores are flat. Frontline staff lack consistency. Service recovery is reactive, not proactive. And competitors are winning on experience, not just price.

Our Approach

Service Culture, Not Just Service Training

Most CX training focuses on scripts and procedures. We go deeper. Our programmes transform the underlying service mindset -- from teller to executive, from receptionist to hospital administrator.

Our signature Service From The Heart (SFTH) methodology was developed specifically for high-stakes service environments in banking and healthcare, where every interaction shapes trust, loyalty, and brand perception.

We combine emotional intelligence development, service recovery frameworks, and customer journey mapping to create a holistic service culture transformation that sticks long after the training ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Service From The Heart is not a customer service skills workshop. It's a mindset transformation programme that changes how your people think about service, not just how they perform it. Generic CX training teaches scripts and procedures; SFTH builds an emotional connection to service excellence that becomes intrinsic to how your team operates. The programme is also industry-specific -- our banking and healthcare versions address the unique challenges and touchpoints of each sector.
We work with your team to establish baseline metrics before the programme begins -- typically NPS, customer satisfaction scores, complaint rates, and service recovery response times. Post-programme, we conduct follow-up assessments at 30, 60, and 90-day intervals to measure behavioural change and business impact. We also provide mystery shopper frameworks and internal audit tools to sustain measurement.
